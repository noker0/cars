Legend Car - Yamaha MT09

Yamaha has been the official engine supplier for the Legend Car since 1993. The current model engine for the Legend Car is the MT09, and is in fact a 3-cylinder motorcycle engine. Following modifications by the U.S. Legend Cars Engine Shop, all engines are sealed and have 135 horsepower before installation in a new build or individually sold. A Legend Car utilizes a spooled rear gear and a six-speed transmission.
